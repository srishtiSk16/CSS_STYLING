Switzerland Multi-Adventure Tour

Swiss Villages & Alpine Bliss in the Bernese Oberland

Length: 6 days / 5 nights
Price:From $4,649 pp dbl. occ.
Regions:Europe At Large

Destinations: Switzerland

Day 1

Savor breathtaking alpine scenery as you commute the Swiss way: by chairlift! | Hike on a ridge above Lake Oeschinen, surrounded by limestone cliffs, snowy peaks and hanging ice | Cold beer and hot cocoa await at a traditional alpine hut.

Day 2

From flower-laden chalets to milk cows adorned with brass bells, the scenery is quintessentially Swiss as you pedal through a serene valley | Ride past teal-colored Lake Thun en route to Interlaken | Hop aboard a cogwheel train bound for Grindelwald | Enjoy an alphorn serenade | Indulge in decadent fondue paired with regional wines.

Days 3-4

Admire the famed Eiger, Mönch and Jungfrau towering above the emerald-green landscape | Hike from First Peak to Bachalpsee, one of Switzerland's most beautiful lakes | Let loose on an exhilarating descent by Trotti bikes into Grindelwald Valley | Walk on the impressive north face of the Eiger Trail, one of Switzerland's most famous hiking routes.

Days 5-6

Bike into Meiringen Valley past countless waterfalls, including seven-tiered Reichenbach Falls | Bike along Lake Brienz and kayak in its turquoise waters | Amble along the lake's shore to the charming town of Iseltwald.

Lodging:

Waldhotel Doldenhorn, Kandersteg, 1 night

A family-run alpine retreat surrounded by verdant forests and soaring snowcapped mountains. A Relais & Châteaux member. (pool, spa)

Romantik Hotel Schweizerhof, Grindelwald, 2 nights

Catch stunning views of Eiger's north face from this luxurious chalet-style hotel. (pool, spa)

Grand Hotel Giessbach, Giessbach, 2 nights

Elegance and old-world charm reign at this turreted beauty in a picturesque setting by Lake Brienz and the Giessbach Falls. (pool)
